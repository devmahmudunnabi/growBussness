Minimalism Just Looks Like This

Living a simple and intentional life has been my goal for a while. I don’t want material sessions and stuff to weigh me down and keep me from doing what I want to do. Over the years I’ve decluttered, given stuff away, and focused on what was important. I feel proud to live a life with stuff and experiences I truly value, I just didn’t expect the anxiety that came from the label of minimalism.
